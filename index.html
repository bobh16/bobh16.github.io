About Me

In 2015, I got my very first camera. From there I started messing around and taking pictures of anything I saw. When I realized that photography had much more that goes into it, I began watching YouTube tutorials and learning more about photography. Shutter speeds, apertures, and focal points made no sense at first but soon enough they became an integral part of my life. Photography has changed the way I view the world, even when I don't have my camera with me, I still imagine I'm looking at things through my lens.
